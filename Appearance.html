Your lovely and loyal companion.

Do you know about the Schnauzer?

Appearance

The characteristics of this dog include：

  • Head:Head firm, square, equal in size in the eyes, oval, dark coloured.
  • Ears:Ears are well developed in high position and of medium thickness. If clipped, the ears should be erect, V-shaped, folded forward, with the inner edges near the cheeks.
  • Eyes:The eyes are medium sized, dark brown, ovate, and directed forwards.
  • Nose:The nose-glasses are large, black and plump, with a double coat of hair.
  • Muzzle:Muzzle strong, parallel to the head and of the same length as the head. The muzzle ends in a blunt wedge shape with exaggerated bristles and whiskers. Is rectangular in appearance throughout the head. A full set of white teeth with a strong and perfect clipped bite .
  • Body:The neck is strong, of medium thickness and length, elegantly curved, and combines briefly with the shoulders. The back is strong and firm. The chest is of moderate width with well expanded ribs and if observed in cross section, should be oval fronted.Legs are thick and strong, with proper angulation of the stifle. The second thigh, the section from the knee to the fly joint, is parallel to the extension of the neck. The feet are round and short, with black claws, a high tail root, and close, slightly arched (cat's foot) toes straight forward. Coat colours are solid black, black and silver, and pretzel.

Styling & Beauty

Styling Style Look
Standard Styles The most common styling of the Schnauzer, the familiar standard look is usually quite simple, just shave the back, leaving the limbs and skirts and feet, while the head is left with only white eyebrows and a beard. The short hairs are cut as short as possible to contrast with the long hairs, giving a stylish look in a refreshing way. Keeping the length of the hair on the belly and limbs, it looks like wearing a small dress, highlighting its playful appearance, making it look like an elegant lady on grand occasions, and chic and charming in outdoor activities.
Poodle or Teddy Some parents may like other breeds of dogs at the same time, the baby at home will be styled to mix and match, such as the Schnauzer's hair for curling, to create a similar style of poodle or Teddy and other styles of styling, personally believe that although it is also not uninteresting, but it is always a departure from the Schnauzer's own skeleton style, half-soft, half-rigid style may be a minority of people's favourite.
The Baby Fat Look This is a look where the Schnauzer retains as much hair as possible on all parts of the body, taking inspiration from physical fashion to create a unique fluffy look. This style is suitable for Schnauzers with ears that hang completely over the cheeks, rounding the hair on the top of the head, with the ear hair hanging down naturally, resembling the Bichon Frise look, which is chubby and cute, but the downside is that it is not easy to take care of on hot summer days, which can aggravate the feeling of discomfort in the baby.
Punk look This type of modelling mainly start with hair accessories and clothing, often with denim clothes and shoes, or to the Schnauzer combed up exaggerated alternative braids to create a relaxed and lively, peculiar and well-behaved European and American punk street style, some of the Europe and the United States punk complex of the rake hemp can try to give the child to produce this modelling, and no loss of adorable.
Clean and sharp look Shave the entire body of the Schnauzer's hair short, the mouth of the hair cut round, looks very fresh and neat, the hair is not easy to knot, after bathing is also relatively fast drying. In addition, shave the tail and shorten the hair on all four legs to make it easier to take care of the Schnauzer. The Schnauzer is a "terrier" and has a hard, natural look, especially on the head and back, so these two areas need to be cut with four different lengths of electric clippers to bring out their features. The side lines on both sides of the body need to be trimmed with scissors to make the side lines look more natural. In addition, the limbs need to be cut into a proportionate cylindrical shape, while the head, eye brows and moustache need to focus on line and length. This short-haired look of the dog is very suitable for owners who love to dress up and take it to different occasions where it can wear different types of clothing and look very sweet and malleable. Since its hair is cut very short, it is okay to trim it only at three months, and the most important thing is to comb the hair of all four legs often.
Cute Panda Styles This look is suitable for light pretzels, the beard and eyebrows are light grey, but there is a circle of fine black eyelashes around the eyes, you can shave all the hair around the eyes to create panda eyes, this look can make the baby's big eyes more adorable sass, a kind of I see you feeling.
Pony suit look This look is also a familiar and favourite look among the public, some people also call it the pony suit look, Schnauzers are usually styled with long hair on the belly that looks like a skirt and a beard on the face. It must have its back shaved, otherwise the hair is too long and creates a messy look. Therefore, its chin hair is cut in a V-shape, its eye brows are cut diagonally in a pointed shape, and the hair in the shoulder area is cut flush along the hair stream to give the appearance of wearing a skirt. As this look retains a certain length of hair on the face, belly and four legs, owners are advised to brush the coat once a day and trim it once a month to maintain the length. You can also leave a strip of horse sideburns on the back.
Arrogant Aristocrat Styling Suitable for thick hair Snow, after repairing the ears, carefully build the eyebrows around the eyes, the rest of the hair can not be repaired, the jaw and chest hair will be complementary to the ear, giving people a proud and noble style.
Alaskan look This look is suitable for black chenari, but requires the beard and legs of the hair is pure white, other parts can not have mixed colours, there is a kind of Eskimo feeling, it should be noted that the owner needs to be often grooming and care, is more need to take care of patience of a kind of shape.
